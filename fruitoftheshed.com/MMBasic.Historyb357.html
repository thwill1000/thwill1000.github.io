The common logarithm function. The natural logarithm of x divided by the natural logarithm of 10.

Logarithms huh?

Syntax:
LOG10(expression)

Example:
x=LOG10(2.88)

Code:
  
	Function Log10(x As Float) As Float
		Log10=Log(x)/2.302585093
	End Function
