Games¶

Boulder Dash

  • Author: "abraxas" with help from "capsikin"
  • Source Repository: https://gitlab.com/raxas/bouldermite/-/tree/master
    • earlier version posted to TBS: https://www.thebackshed.com/forum/uploads/capsikin/2020-07-28_232351_BDASH.zip

Falfus 2

A puzzle game based on Puzznic. The goal is to clear each level by sliding matching tiles together. Two or more of the same tile touching along any edge will fuse and vanish with a pop. The player can slide tiles sideways only, and tiles fall when unsupported.
  • Download: https://www.thebackshed.com/forum/ViewTopic.php?TID=12248
  • Author: "vegipete"

Flappy Bird

Simple Flappy Bird clone.
  • Download: http://www.thebackshed.com/forum/ViewTopic.php?TID=12363
  • Author: "jeff510"

  • Source Repository: https://github.com/mauroxavierneto/gauntlet_cmm2

Gomoku

  • Download: http://www.thebackshed.com/forum/ViewTopic.php?FID=16&TID=12448

Lunar Lander

  • Download: https://www.thebackshed.com/forum/ViewTopic.php?TID=12303&PID=150642#150642

Max-E-Man

  • Download: http://www.thebackshed.com/forum/ViewTopic.php?FID=16&TID=12415#151083

Reverse

A puzzle game in which the player must arrange a list of numbers in numerical order from left to right. Each move the player tells the computer how many numbers in the list (counting from the left) to reverse.
  • Download: http://www.thebackshed.com/forum/ViewTopic.php?TID=12357&PID=150315#150315
  • Author: "vegipete" from an original article by Peter Sessions

Sudoku

  • Download: http://www.thebackshed.com/forum/ViewTopic.php?TID=12159&PID=146887#146887

Wolfenstein 3D

WARNING! May contain Nazis, swastikas and violence.
  • Author: Mauro Xavier Neto
  • Source Repository: https://github.com/mauroxavierneto/wolf3d_cmm2

Z-MIM

A Z-machine interpreter allowing the classic Infocom works of interactive fiction (e.g. Zork I, II & III) to be played on the Colour Maximite 2 - includes "Mini-Zork" sample game.
  • Download: https://github.com/thwill1000/zmim/releases/download/r3/zmim-r3.zip (Release 3)
  • Author: Tom Williams "thwill" (https://www.sockpuppetstudios.com)
  • Source Repository: https://github.com/thwill1000/zmim
