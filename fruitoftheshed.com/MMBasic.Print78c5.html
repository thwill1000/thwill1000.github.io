Eaten Earth

Modified on 2019/01/13 08:07 by Sazazel — Categorized as: Graphics

Short Animation Demo to show animations of large multi-tile sprites and pseudo scrolling.
Exits to manual mode so you can play around with the various sprite animations.

Unzip the file and all the needed files are there.

Enjoy :)
